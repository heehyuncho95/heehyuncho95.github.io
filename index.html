Arduino Caliper

A caliper-like instrument was developed to measure the dimensions of the Lego blocks. The 28BYJ-48 stepper motor was used as the sensor and the signal modification system were the rack and pinion gears that converted the rotary movement of the motor into a linear movement system. This allowed the heads of the caliper to tightly fit the blocks in between to be measured. Finally, for the recorder and indicator, the dimensions of the blocks were determined by using the code implemented in Arduino and was displayed on a computer monitor.

Davinci Printer and Scanner

Davinci Printer and Scanner was created using the Lego Mindstorms Kit to perform the functions of a multifunctional printer with the concept of Neo Pointillism. The device was created to showcase how a group of dots could be brought together to create artwork. This technique also allowed viewers to use their eyes to connect the dots and formulate a picture in their mind. The device was able to print text and images, scan simple images to a file viewable on a computer and photocopy simple images, all through the use of dots.
